Published by Haribhakti Education Foundation

Haribhakti Education Foundation is a visionary initiative dedicated to advancing medical education, surgical innovation, and skill development for the next generation of surgeons. An initiative of Dr. Sanjiv Haribhakti, and founded with a commitment to excellence, the foundation supports high-quality training programs, academic collaborations, digital learning platforms, and the dissemination of cutting-edge surgical knowledge. Under its umbrella, several surgical books have been released to empower learners and practitioners with authoritative resources.

Additionally, Haribhakti Education foundation operates the website gisurgery.info, a virtual surgical repository accessible worldwide, offering free reference materials, procedural guides, and educational tools for GI-surgeons and trainees. This website helps individuals and families understand gastrointestinal diseases, treatment options, preventive health, nutrition, and postoperative care—promoting informed decision-making and improved outcomes.

Kaizen Hospital – Stands for continuous progressive improvement

Kaizen Hospital stands as one of India’s most trusted and advanced institutions for Gastrointestinal, HPB, and Minimally Invasive Surgery. Built on the philosophy of continuous improvement—the true meaning of “Kaizen”—our hospital has redefined standards of precision, safety, and patient-centered care. We continuously innovate to proficiently use the most advanced technologies in the most judicious manner to enhance patient recovery and outcomes. This is what makes Kaizen a Centre of Excellence in Robotic Gi Surgery in India

1. Gujarat’s First Super-Speciality Hospital Dedicated to Gastroenterology

Kaizen Hospital was established with a singular vision: to provide comprehensive, specialised care exclusively for digestive diseases. This focus allows us to offer unmatched expertise across the entire spectrum of GI, liver, pancreas, and biliary conditions.

2. Leadership by Renowned Experts

Under the leadership of Dr. Sanjiv Haribhakti, one of India’s foremost GI, HPB and Colorectal surgeon, and a pioneer in robotic GI surgery, our team brings decades of clinical excellence, innovation, and compassionate care. Our consultants are nationally recognised leaders who contribute to research, training, and surgical advancement.

3. Advanced Robotic & Minimally Invasive Surgery Program

Kaizen is one of the few centres in India with a robust robotic surgery program, completing 200+ successful robotic GI procedures using India’s advanced indigenous robotic platform, the SSi Mantra 3.0. With high-definition 3D vision, tremor filtration, and unmatched precision.

  • Smaller incisions
  • Less pain and blood loss
  • Lower complications
  • Faster recobery
  • Superior outcomes in complex surgeries

    • 4. Comprehensive Care Under One Roof

    From diagnosis to minimally invasive treatment and long-term follow-up, Kaizen offers a fully integrated ecosystem:

  • State-of-the-art operating rooms
  • Endoscopy & ERCP suites
  • Advanced imaging – including CT Scan
  • Functional Labs – 24 hr pH Impedance monitoring & Manometry
  • Intensive care and recovery units
  • Nutritional, rehabilitation and counselling services

    • This seamless coordination ensures accuracy, safety, and convenience for every patient.

    5. Excellence in GI Oncology & Complex Surgeries

    Kaizen Hospital is one of the few centres capable of handling highly complex GI cancers, liver resections, pancreatic surgeries, major colorectal procedures, and re-operative cases with outstanding outcomes. Our multi-disciplinary discussions and evidence-based decision making ensures personalised care.
