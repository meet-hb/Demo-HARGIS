Kaizen Hospital also offers short-term observorships for Indian and foreign medical students and young surgeons.

Participants can:

  • Learn clinical decision-making in a dedicated GI centre
  • Experience high-volume surgical practice in a private hospital in India
  • Interact closely with consultants and the academic team

  • Academic maturity and scientific curiosity
